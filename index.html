Become the Heart of Your Community

Welcome to Shaktipreneur — a one-of-a-kind platform that transforms women residents into powerful community leaders. Whether you're resolving everyday issues, ensuring the society looks its best, or being the first face a new family sees — you’re not just helping, you’re leading.

Here, every woman is a changemaker. And every effort earns recognition.

What is a Shaktipreneur?

A Shaktipreneur is :

  • The bridge between residents and facility teams
  • The voice that listens, represents, and solves
  • The guardian of cleanliness, warmth, and community spirit
  • The leader who sets the tone for how the society lives, grows, and thrives

Why join the program?

By becoming a Shaktipreneur, you get:

Recognition for your leadership in society life

A voice in decisions that shape your community

Skill-building through soft training in leadership and coordination

Appreciation & rewards for consistent contribution

Belonging to a growing family of empowered Sikka women

You're not just joining a program. You're becoming a part of the Sikka family — committed to building better, more beautiful communities together.
